# Design Tokens

## Visual identity
- Light mode only.
- Primary: `#0b2c40`.
- Background: `#fafafa`.
- Logo font: Lobster.

## Accent palette (approved, pastel-leaning)
- Forest: `#04261f`
- Slate: `#213448`
- Teal Deep: `#005461`
- Navy: `#1b3c53`
- Gray Blue: `#435663`
- Teal: `#427a76`
- Red (alert only): `#ff5555`
- Red Muted (alert only): `#d34e4e`

## Usage limits
- Max 2 accent colors per screen.
- Accents are for backgrounds, borders, soft gradients, or subtle blobs.
- Avoid neon or high-saturation colors.
- Reds are reserved for error/alert states only.
- Do not use accent colors in large solid blocks; keep intensity low.

## Spacing
- Base 4px scale: 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32, 40, 48.
- Prefer `p-4`, `p-6`, `p-8` and `gap-4`, `gap-6`.
- Section spacing: `py-12` or `py-16`.

## Surfaces
- Section backgrounds: `bg-background`, `bg-background/50`, `bg-white/50`.
- Borders: `border` + `border-primary/5` for subtle separation.
- Cards: `rounded-xl` or `rounded-2xl`, `shadow-sm` or `shadow-lg`.

## Typography
- Body: Inter.
- Logo: Lobster.
- Headings: `text-3xl`, `text-2xl`, `text-xl`, `text-lg`.
- Body: `text-base`.

## Notes
- Keep colors within the palette.
- No custom spacing values without explicit approval.
